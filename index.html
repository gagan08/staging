Welcome to Aquuamarine

we use professional industrial standard equipment and food grade chemicals that are lab tested and marked safe for domestic usage for cleaning your space. This ensures quality and peace of mind to each of our customers.

Aquuamarine was founded in July, 2015 with a clear vision to take home cleaning and sanitisation to a whole new level and to create awareness about how cleanliness and sanitisation can benefit the community. Since then, our mission is to reach more number of residences and provide professional cleaning services.

We have a great team of professionally trained staff backing us to help us achieve our mission. They have an eye for detail and make sure cleaning of all the problem areas are addressed. They are extremely professional with handling your personal belongings; result oriented and aim to make their visit a pleasant one. They follow the prescribed procedures and complete the tasks assigned in order to achieve the quality you deserve.

We go beyond cleaning and provide new age sanitizing solutions. We follow a 2 step process – Deep Vacuuming and Sanitization. The process starts with first sanitizing the local surface and next a deep vacuum cleaner that has a wooden roller is used which beats the dust to the surface and this is vacuumed immediately. A water canister collects the dust particles and only the clean air is pumped back out. Our “Double check” procedure ensures the highest level of hygiene is maintained.
